> Question

* Outputs the remainder of the product of N integers per line divided by M

```txt
Input:
10 9999
100 100 100 100 100 100 100 100 100 100

Output: 1
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_15818.py' %}

```py
d = input().split()[1]
print(eval(input().replace(" ","*")) % int(d))
```
