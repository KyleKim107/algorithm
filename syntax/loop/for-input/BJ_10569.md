> Question

* Find number of faces of convex polyhedrons for Vertex and Edge

```txt
Input:
2
8 12
4 6

Output:
6
4
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_10569.py' %}

```py
for _ in range(int(input())):
  V, E = map(int, input().split())
  print(2 - V + E)
```
