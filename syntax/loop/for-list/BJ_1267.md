> Question

* Type 1 : 10 won every 30 (starts from 1)
* Type 2 : 60 won every 15 (starts from 1)
* print chepare one

```txt
Input:
3
40 40 40

Output: M 45
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_1267.py' %}

```py
input()
y = m = 0
for p in map(int, input().split()):
  y += p // 30 * 10 + 10
  m += p // 60 * 15 + 15
print('M' if y > m else 'Y' if y<m else 'Y M', min(y, m))
```
