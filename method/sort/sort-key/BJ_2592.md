> Question

* find the average and the lowest value of ten natural numbers

```txt
Input:
10
40
30
60
30
20
60
30
40
50

Output: 37 30
```

```py
numbers = [int(input()) for i in range(10)]
print(sum(numbers)//10)
print(max(numbers, key = numbers.count))
```
