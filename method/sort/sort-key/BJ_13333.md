> Question

* Print k index
* Of n papers published, k if more than k papers cited and remaining n-k papers cited is less than k

```txt
Input:
5
8 4 5 3 10

Output: 4
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_13333.py' %}

```py
input()
q = 0
for e in sorted(map(int,input().split()), reverse=True):
  q += e > q
print(q)
```
