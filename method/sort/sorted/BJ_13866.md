> Question

* Given four numbers, find minimum difference of two pair

```txt
Input: 4 7 10 20
Output: 7
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_13866.py' %}

```py
a, b, c, d = sorted(map(int, input().split()))
print(abs(d + a - (b + c)))
```
