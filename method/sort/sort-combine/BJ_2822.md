> Question

* Print sum of top 5 scores and their index in sorted order

```txt
Input:
20
30
50
48
33
66
0
64

Output:
261
3 4 5 6 8
```

{% endtab %}
{% tab title='BJ_2822.py' %}

```py
a = [(int(input()), i+1) for i in range(8)]
s = sorted(a)[-5:]
print(sum(p[0] for p in s))
print(*(sorted(p[1] for p in s)))
```
