> Question

* Given two lists, find maximum sum points when you cannot take two adjacent coin

```txt
Input:
2
5
50 10 100 20 40
30 50 70 10 60
7
10 30 10 50 100 20 40
20 40 30 50 60 20 80

Output:
260
290
```

for _ in range(int(input())):
  n = int(input())
  A = [list(map(int,input().split())) for _ in range(2)]
  top, down, no = 0, 0, 0
  for i in range(n):
