> Question

* Find maximum number of island after all node smaller than some N are deleted

```txt
Input:
5
6 8 2 6 2
3 2 3 4 6
6 7 3 3 2
7 2 5 3 6
8 9 5 2 7

Output: 5
```

n = int(input())
G = [[*map(int,input().split())] for i in[0]*n]
